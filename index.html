Our Environmental Policy

HL Steel Industries Sdn. Bhd. values environmental care as part of our daily operations. We strive to maintain a clean, safe, and responsible working environment that aligns with the guidelines from Jabatan Alam Sekitar (JAS).

  • Handle, store, and label scheduled wastes properly according to environmental guidelines.
  • Encourage waste reduction and recycling activities to minimize environmental impact.
  • Improve workplace cleanliness, safety, and environmental awareness among all employees.
  • Monitor operational practices and promote continuous improvement towards sustainable work processes.

Environmental Initiatives

1. Scheduled Waste Management

We strictly follow JAS guidelines for storing, labeling, and disposing of scheduled wastes. All hazardous materials are handled by trained personnel to minimize environmental risks.

2. Waste Reduction & Recycling

HL Steel promotes a culture of recycling in our daily operations. Recyclable materials are collected, sorted, and sent for proper processing to reduce landfill impact.

3. Clean and Safe Workplace

Regular cleaning schedules, proper storage of materials, and awareness programs ensure our workplace is safe and environmentally friendly.

4. Employee Engagement

Employees are regularly trained on environmental practices and encouraged to contribute ideas for sustainability projects, promoting collective responsibility.
